# Why this topic matters

You cannot describe motion without calculus. Position is integrated velocity, which is integrated acceleration. Robotic motion is governed by ODEs. Stability analysis lives in the frequency domain. Lagrangian mechanics gives you a principled way to derive the equations of motion of a multi-link robot.

## Deep-dive subtopics

- **Limits, derivatives, integration.** The basics, but kept connected to physical interpretation.
- **Continuous optimization.** Gradient descent, Newton steps, constrained optimization basics.
- **Ordinary differential equations.** Linear and nonlinear systems; numerical integration (Euler, RK4).
- **Laplace transforms & Lagrangian mechanics.** The control engineer's toolkit and the energy-based view of dynamics.

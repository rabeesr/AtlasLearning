# Why this topic matters

You should not reinvent the wheel for inter-process communication, message serialization, simulation, and visualization. ROS 2 is the standard. Knowing it well lets you focus on the parts of robotics that are actually hard.

## Deep-dive subtopics

- **Architecture.** DDS, QoS profiles, lifecycle nodes, namespaces.
- **Nodes, topics, services, actions.** The core communication primitives.
- **rviz & Gazebo.** Visualize state, simulate physics, drive sensors.

## What comes next

ROS 2 is the connective tissue for the rest of the curriculum. Manipulator simulation, mobile-robot stacks, and perception pipelines all sit on top of it.

# Why this topic matters

Probabilistic robotics is the meeting point of estimation, optimization, and analysis. To work confidently in this space, you need fluency with abstract linear algebra, projection-based estimation theory, real analysis, and nonlinear optimization.

## Deep-dive subtopics

- **Abstract linear algebra.** Vector spaces, inner products, orthogonal bases, Gram-Schmidt.
- **Projection theorem & BLUE.** Best linear unbiased estimators; the bridge from least squares to Kalman filtering.
- **Real analysis.** Cauchy sequences, continuous functions, the Weierstrass theorem.
- **Nonlinear optimization.** Newton-Raphson, line search, trust regions.

# Why this topic matters

LIDAR gives you geometry. Cameras give you meaning. Modern robots use both. Perception turns pixels into the semantic, geometric, and dynamic structure a robot needs to act intelligently.

## Deep-dive subtopics

- **Classical vision.** Pinhole cameras, calibration, projective geometry, feature extraction (SIFT, ORB).
- **Modern deep learning.** Object detection (YOLO), semantic segmentation, drivable-space detection.
- **Visual odometry.** Recovering camera motion from frame-to-frame correspondences.

## What comes next

Perception, SLAM, and ROS 2 together form the autonomy stack of most modern mobile robots.

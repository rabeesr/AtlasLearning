# PID as a practical foothold

PID control is often one of the first controllers engineers implement because it is simple enough to understand end to end, yet powerful enough to solve many real problems.

## Three terms, three jobs

- Proportional reacts to current error.
- Integral reacts to accumulated past error.
- Derivative reacts to how quickly the error is changing.

PID is a strong bridge between theory and coding. It is a natural place to connect lecture notes, a browser-based challenge, and future spaced-repetition quizzes.
